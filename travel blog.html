Stop 1

The City of Lakes

Udaipur

Udaipur stole my heart with its shimmering lakes, majestic palaces, and romantic sunsets. The City Palace towering over Lake Pichola felt like stepping into a fairy tale. Every corner of this city whispers stories of Rajput royalty and timeless beauty.

City Palace Lake Pichola Jag Mandir Saheliyon Ki Bari
Stop 2
↓

The Fort of Valor

Chittorgarh

Chittorgarh Fort stands as a monument to Rajput courage and sacrifice. Walking through its massive gates and ancient ruins, I could almost hear the echoes of battles fought centuries ago. The Vijay Stambh at sunset was an unforgettable sight.

Chittorgarh Fort Vijay Stambh Padmini Palace
Stop 3

The City of Temples

Ujjain

Ujjain is one of the holiest cities in India. Visiting the Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga was a deeply spiritual experience. The evening aarti on the banks of the Shipra River filled the air with devotion and peace.

Mahakaleshwar Temple Ram Ghat Shipra River
